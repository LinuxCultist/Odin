Lasgna

Description

Lasagna, also known by the plural form lasagne, is a type of pasta made in very wide, flat sheets.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Meat
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onion & Garlic
  • herbs & Spices
  • Eggs
  • Olive oil or butter

Steps

  1. Prep the sauce
  2. Mix the ricotta layer
  3. Cook the noodles
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake it
  6. Rest and serve
